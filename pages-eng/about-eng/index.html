Su di me

I am Daniele Lessio from Treviso, Italy, a graduate in Graphic and Multimedia Communication Sciences.

For the past 3 years I have been studying and working as a graphic designer, gaining experience in both web-side and print-side content creation.

While working, I deepened my knowledge of the Wordpress CMS and honed my skills in teaming up on projects intended for printing and papermaking...

I have good command of the Adobe suite for my creative projects, but I also love to freehand illustrate projects that need more character and experiment with 3D.
